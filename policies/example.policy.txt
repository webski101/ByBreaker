# Agent Circuit Compiler — example risk policy
#
# One rule per line: <ACTION> IF <METRIC> <OP> <VALUE>[%] [OVER <N> TRADES]
# ACTION: WARN | PAUSE | HALT | KILL   (severity increases in that order)
# Lines starting with # are comments. Case-insensitive.

# --- Drawdown: whole-equity-curve, peak vs current ---
WARN IF drawdown > 3%
PAUSE IF drawdown > 8%
HALT IF drawdown > 15%

# --- Concentration: is the agent diversified or all-in on one symbol? ---
WARN IF hhi > 0.5 OVER 15 TRADES
HALT IF hhi > 0.75 OVER 15 TRADES
HALT IF exposurePct > 80% OVER 15 TRADES

# --- Win rate: is the strategy still working? ---
PAUSE IF winRate < 25% OVER 30 TRADES

# --- Hard USD loss limits, reset at UTC midnight ---
HALT IF dailyLossUsd > 800
KILL IF dailyLossUsd > 2000

# --- Runaway-loop detection: an agent firing trades faster than a human
#     could sanity-check is treated as a bug, not a strategy ---
WARN IF tradeFrequency > 10
HALT IF tradeFrequency > 25
